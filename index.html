The Lhasa Apso has a very calm temperament, polite and obedient, is considered an easy race to control. She has a smooth and long coat, which if she prefers it may be sheared, but usually does not need coughing. They are known to be protective, this instinct is turned on 24 hours a day.

Eurasier

The Eurasier is a medium-sized dog with a thick, medium-length coat that can present a variety of colors. Confident, calm and balanced, he is loyal to the whole family, but reserved for strangers. He should live in close contact with his family as it is not suitable to be kept in kennels or strapped outside.
